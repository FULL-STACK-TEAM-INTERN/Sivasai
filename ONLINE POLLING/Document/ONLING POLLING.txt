                                                                  ONLINE POLLING(Revision-1)
ABSTRACT:
 
This project is about percentage polling through know the opinion of the customers. 
In this project the main agenda of the project is to show the opion about the people of social media , products,reviews etc.. through polling. 
In this project we will going to show the opinions of the people in percentage module.

•	Viewing data
